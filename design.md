# BK AI Systems — Design Direction

## Aesthetic
Dark industrial command-center. Cyber-industrial AI automation mood.

## Colors
- Background: `#050c1a` (near black navy)
- Surface: `#0a1628` (dark navy panels)
- Border: `#1a2f50` (subtle panel borders)
- Primary accent: `#1e6fff` (electric blue)
- Secondary accent: `#38beff` (cyan)
- Glow: `rgba(56,190,255,0.15)` (subtle panel glow)
- Text primary: `#f0f6ff`
- Text muted: `#6b8cbe`
- Danger/alert: `#ff4757`

## Typography
- Display/Headlines: `'Barlow Condensed'`, bold, uppercase, condensed
- Body: `'IBM Plex Mono'` for technical labels; `'Inter'` for readable body
- Micro-labels: IBM Plex Mono, 10-11px, letter-spacing wide, muted blue

## Texture & Effects
- Subtle CSS grid overlay on background (thin lines, low opacity)
- Scanline texture via repeating-linear-gradient
- Panel glow via box-shadow with cyan
- Corner bracket decorations (CSS pseudo-elements or SVG)
- Animated pulse on accent elements

## Layout
- One-page, full-width sections
- Sharp angular clipped buttons (clip-path)
- Thin 1px borders on cards/panels
- Corner bracket `[ ]` details on section headers
- Terminal-style mono labels above section titles

## Sections Order
1. NAV
2. HERO
3. PROBLEM
4. SOLUTION
5. WHAT WE BUILD
6. HOW IT WORKS
7. RESULTS / STATS
8. TESTIMONIALS
